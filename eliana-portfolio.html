I'm a General Management student at Purdue University with a concentration in Management Consulting — and a genuine obsession with how brands show up online.

I've been making content since 2021 — starting as a TikTok brand ambassador for iBlason, then growing into YouTube creation and series hosting for Purdue's Boiler Ambassadors program, and now leading social media for Alpha Kappa Psi as VP of Public Relations.

I bring a rare mix of creative instinct and analytical thinking to every project. I know how to build content systems, write hooks that hold attention, and actually read the data to know what's working. And I work digitally — so I can help you from anywhere.
